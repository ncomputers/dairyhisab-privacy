Introduction

DairyHisab ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our dairy management mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

  • Name and contact details when you create an account
  • Business information including business name, address, and contact details
  • User credentials (username and encrypted password)

2. Business Data

  • Milk collection records (quantity, quality, dates)
  • Customer information (names, contact details, transaction history)
  • Financial data (payments, invoices, balances)
  • Inventory records (stock levels, product details)
  • Business reports and analytics

3. Technical Data

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Usage data (app features used, time spent in app)
  • Log data (app performance, error reports)

4. Location Data

  • Business location (address for delivery/service areas)
  • GPS coordinates (only with explicit user consent)

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purpose

  • Provide dairy management services as requested
  • Process transactions and generate reports
  • Manage customer relationships and communications
  • Maintain business records and historical data

Secondary Purpose

  • Improve app functionality and user experience

  • Analyze usage patterns for business optimization
  • Send important notifications about your account

Data Storage and Security

Storage Methods

Security Measures

Data Sharing

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Limited Sharing
