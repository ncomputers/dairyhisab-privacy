Introduction

DairyHisab ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our dairy management mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Minimal Technical Data

  • Device information (device type, operating system) - for app compatibility
  • App performance data (crash reports, error logs) - for bug fixing only
  • Basic usage statistics (app opens, feature usage) - for improvement

2. What We DON'T Collect

  • No personal information - we don't collect names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No business data - your milk records, customer data, financial info stays private
  • No location data - we don't track your location
  • No contacts - we don't access your phone contacts
  • No photos/files - we don't access your personal files

3. User-Controlled Data

  • Business records (milk collection, payments, inventory) - stored locally only
  • Customer information - stored locally only
  • Financial data - stored locally only
  • App settings - stored locally only

How We Use Your Information

What We Use

  • Device information to ensure app compatibility and fix bugs
  • Performance data to improve app stability and performance
  • Basic usage stats to understand which features are most useful

What We DON'T Use

  • No business data analysis - we never access or analyze your business records
  • No customer data processing - your customer information stays private
  • No financial data access - we never see or process your payment records
  • No marketing - we don't use your data for advertising or promotions

Local Processing Only

  • All business operations happen on your device only
  • No data transmission to our servers
  • No cloud processing of your business information
  • Complete offline functionality - app works without internet

Data Storage and Security

Storage Methods

  • Local storage only on your device for complete privacy

Our Privacy Promise
